# Product Creation & Edit App

A simple and efficient application for creating, editing, and managing products in your inventory. Designed to streamline product entry, maintain accurate records, and improve workflow for businesses of any size.

---

## Features

- **Add New Products**: Quickly create new products with details like name, SKU, price, barcode, category, and stock quantity.  
- **Edit Existing Products**: Update product details easily without affecting other records.  
- **Search & Filter**: Find products quickly by name, SKU, or barcode.  
- **Bulk Actions**: Option to update multiple products at once (if supported).  
- **Validation & Error Handling**: Ensures required fields are filled and prevents duplicate entries.  
- **Integration Ready**: Can be connected to inventory, POS, or e-commerce systems.
